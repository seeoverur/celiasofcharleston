About This Guide

Charleston, SC

Charleston Table Co. is a recommendation website. We're an independent guide to boutique culinary studios and catering companies serving Charleston, SC. We're not affiliated with, owned by, or operated by any of the businesses listed here.

Our listings are based on local reputation, reviews, and general knowledge of Charleston's boutique food scene. Menus, pricing, and availability can change, so we always recommend reaching out directly to a business before booking.

Think of this site as a starting point for finding a smaller, chef-driven kitchen for your next event — not a booking service, just a guide.
