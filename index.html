Boutique Food, Done Right

Charleston, SC

A curated guide to Charleston's small-batch culinary studios and boutique catering companies — the kind of kitchens that treat every event like it's the only one on the calendar.

Chef-Driven

Boutique Culinary Studios

Small kitchens led by chefs, not corporate menus — built around personal service, seasonal ingredients, and a smaller client list by design.

Iron Clay Catering

Personalized chef services in your own kitchen

A small, boutique studio led by chef Travis Grimes, offering personal chef services and custom menus for weddings, corporate gatherings, and intimate dinner parties, built on two decades of relationships with local farmers and fishermen.

Specialty
Personal chef experiences
Best For
Intimate dinners & private events

Cozy Kitchen by Chef Katie Murdock

Custom menus built around your story

Led by Chef Katie Murdock, this studio builds custom wedding and event menus around each client's personal style, drawing on more than 20 years of culinary experience.

Specialty
Custom wedding menus
Best For
Personalized wedding catering

Sweet Palm Private Events

Coffee shop roots, full event menus

Based out of a beloved coffee shop just off King Street, Sweet Palm Private Events brings that same made-from-scratch approach to full catering menus, serving events at the customer's chosen location.

Specialty
Coffee-shop-driven event menus
Best For
Off-site private events

Sweet Palm Coffee & Food

The King Street kitchen behind Sweet Palm's catering

The everyday storefront behind Sweet Palm Private Events, serving breakfast and brunch plates, hearty lunch sandwiches, and wraps like the chicken caesar wrap and boca wrap, plus egg bites for the grab-and-go crowd. It's the same made-from-scratch kitchen that scales up for private events and catering.

Specialty
Breakfast, brunch & lunch
Best For
Everyday plates from the same kitchen
Full-Service

Boutique Catering Companies

Established catering houses that still operate at a boutique scale — custom menus, hands-on planning, and a name locals actually recognize.

Rosemary & Rye

Female-founded, full-service catering

Founded in 2012 and led by chef Meghan Timby, Rosemary & Rye has grown into one of Charleston's premier full-service catering companies, known for exquisite cuisine paired with genuine Southern hospitality.

Specialty
Full-service event catering
Best For
Weddings & large events

Harvest Catering

Seasonal menus from Lowcountry farms

Built by business partners Lexie Webb and Ryan Sullivan around their shared hospitality and culinary backgrounds, Harvest Catering sources fresh, seasonal ingredients from local farmers and fishermen for an ever-changing menu.

Specialty
Seasonal, farm-driven menus
Best For
Locally-sourced event menus

Caroline's Market & Catering

A Charleston catering institution

With decades of experience in Charleston, Caroline's Market & Catering stands as one of the city's premier boutique catering companies, also running a daily market case of pizza, quiche, chicken salad, and casseroles to go.

Specialty
Full-service catering & market case
Best For
Events & grab-and-go meals

Relish Distinctive Catering

Tricounty boutique catering since 2005

Serving the Tricounty area since 2005, Relish Distinctive Catering offers a boutique catering and bar experience with menus designed around each client's taste, place, and occasion.

Specialty
Catering & bar service
Best For
Weddings & social events
